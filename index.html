Welcome to our website, where we strive to provide compassionate and comprehensive care to our patients. Our team includes experienced and skilled gynecologists and dentists, who are dedicated to helping our patients maintain their reproductive and oral health

Our gynecology practice offers a wide range of services, including routine exams, Pap smears, STD testing, birth control counseling, and menopause management. We understand that visiting the gynecologist can be uncomfortable for some patients, which is why we work hard to create a welcoming and non-judgmental environment. Our gynecologists take the time to listen to our patients' concerns and answer any questions they may have, ensuring that they feel informed and empowered throughout their care.

Our dentistry practice provides a full range of dental services, including routine cleanings, fillings, crowns, and root canals. We believe that oral health is an essential component of overall health, and we work closely with our patients to develop personalized treatment plans that meet their unique needs. Our dentists are skilled in the latest techniques and use state-of-the-art equipment to ensure that our patients receive the best possible care.

At our practice, we are committed to providing our patients with the highest level of care. We believe that education is a critical component of preventive medicine, and we work closely with our patients to help them understand their bodies and take an active role in their health. We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality healthcare, and we are proud to accept a wide range of insurance plans.

Thank you for considering our practice for your gynecology and dentistry needs. We look forward to partnering with you on your health journey.

Established in 2002, Nairobi care has been a pillar of the Nairobi community for over 20+ years. Founded by a group of visionary healthcare professionals, our hospital was created to provide top-quality medical care to patients from all walks of life. Over the years, we have grown to become a top-notch facility offering a wide range of medical services, including emergency care, surgery, maternity care, and oncology.
